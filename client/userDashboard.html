Mental Health Awareness

Mental health is the foundation of our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It shapes how we think, feel, act, and interact with others. Prioritizing mental health helps us manage stress, build strong relationships, and make healthy choices throughout life.

Why Mental Health Matters

01

Personal Impact

Impacts relationships, productivity, and physical health

02

Community Benefits

Reduces stigma and encourages open conversations

03

Support Network

Empowers people to seek help and support

04

Economic Impact

Reduces healthcare costs and improves workplace performance

Common Challenges

Anxiety & Stress

Persistent worry, tension, or nervousness that can affect daily life.

Depression

Ongoing sadness, loss of interest, or hopelessness that lasts for weeks or longer.

Burnout

Emotional exhaustion from prolonged stress, often related to work or caregiving.

Social Isolation

Feeling disconnected or alone, which can impact mental and physical health.

  Important Notice for Kenya

If you're experiencing a mental health emergency in Kenya:

  • Call 999 for Kenya Police emergency services
  • Call Befrienders Kenya: +254 722 178 177 (24/7 suicide prevention)
  • Visit your nearest public hospital emergency department
  • Contact Amref Health Africa: +254 703 037 000

These resources are for informational purposes only and do not constitute medical advice. Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional for proper diagnosis and treatment. Services may vary by location within Kenya.
